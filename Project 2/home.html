Meal Finder

An easy way to look for recipes!


What does the app do?

The app can be used to find recipes for meals with little to no effort! All the data you need will be displayed!

How do I use it?

You can search for a meal by name. The meals can be sorted by number of results, the category of the meal or the cuisine that it belongs to

Why should I use it?

Have you ever looked for something to cook but couldn't find a recipe that isn't prefaced without 3 pages of the author's backstory? Well I have and I hate it. This makes life much easier

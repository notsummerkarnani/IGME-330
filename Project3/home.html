Shadow Boxer

A boxing game that uses the webcam for input!


How do I play?

  1. Go to the App page and let it load
  2. Make sure you allow access to the webcam.
  3. Hit "Play"
  4. Box Away!

What makes the game unique?

Have you ever played a game without a controller? Okay maybe you played on an XBOX Kinect... But it's not really portable, is it? Now you can take your boxing tenencies on the fly!

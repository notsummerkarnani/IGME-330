Shadow Boxer

A boxing game that uses the webcam for input!


How do I play?

  1. Go to the App page
  2. Press the Setup Webcam button to set up the webcam
  3. Figure out the optimal space to stand in, so that your whole upper body is visible
  4. Hit "Play"
  5. Box Away!

What makes the game unique?

Have you ever played a game without a controller? Okay maybe you played on an XBOX Kinect... But it's not really portable, is it? Now you can take your boxing tendencies on the fly!

Why should I play it?

Have you ever wanted to do some boxing but didn't want to leave the comfort of your home? Well now you can! This state of the art simulation allows you to practice your punches with virtual enemies
